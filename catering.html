Catering Specials


Catering is a service we offer to our customers that allows for you to easily provide our food to large groups of people with bulk orders and large trays

Catering offers an adapted menu, suited to large feast, with specialities including, a make it your self station, a variety of trays based chosen by yourself, and large meals based on different cuisines


Offering diverse and flavorful vegetarian catering options demonstrates a commitment to inclusivity and caters to the growing demand for plant-based choices, enhancing your business's appeal to a wider clientele.

Vegetarian catering provides a vibrant and interactive culinary experience for parties, ensuring all guests, regardless of dietary restrictions, can enjoy delicious and satisfying food.

A thoughtfully curated vegetarian wedding menu adds a unique and memorable touch, reflecting modern values and accommodating diverse guest needs while maintaining elegance and sophistication.

Large-Format Vegetable Sushi Platter: Create a visually stunning and communal dining experience with a large-format Japanese "Vegetable Sushi Platter," designed to serve twelve guests. This elaborate platter features a variety of meticulously crafted vegetarian sushi rolls, including creamy avocado rolls, crisp cucumber rolls, sweet inari pockets filled with seasoned sushi rice, and crunchy vegetable tempura rolls. Accompanying the sushi would be a generous portion of vibrant seaweed salad (wakame), thinly sliced pickled ginger (gari) for palate cleansing, and a dollop of fiery wasabi. Offer a selection of high-quality soy sauces, including a low-sodium option, and a spicy chili sauce for dipping, enhancing the flavorful and interactive dining experience.





Create an engaging, interactive meal with a "Build-Your-Own Asian Lettuce Wraps" station. Offer a variety of bases: crisp butter lettuce cups, delicate rice paper wrappers, and fluffy steamed buns. Fillings include marinated and grilled tofu with a spicy Korean gochujang glaze, a savory mushroom stir-fry featuring shiitake and oyster mushrooms in a ginger-soy sauce, and tender jackfruit rendang, slow-cooked in a rich coconut curry. A vibrant array of toppings, such as pickled carrots and daikon, fresh cilantro  and mint, toasted peanuts, and a selection of sauces like a creamy peanut sauce, a tangy sweet chili glaze, and a fragrant ginger-soy  dressing, allows guests to personalize their wraps.





Vegetarian Cuisine Trays: Design a diverse and flavorful catering spread with individual vegetarian trays, each highlighting a distinct cuisine. An Indian tray could feature crispy vegetable samosas served with sweet and tangy tamarind chutney, succulent paneer tikka skewers marinated in a fragrant yogurt and spice blend, and a comforting aloo gobi (potato and cauliflower curry) accompanied by warm, freshly baked naan bread. A North African tray might showcase a fragrant vegetable tagine, slow-cooked with apricots, prunes, and almonds, a refreshing couscous salad tossed with roasted vegetables and fresh herbs, and crispy spiced chickpea fritters (falafel) served with a cooling yogurt-mint sauce. An East Asian tray could include delicate vegetable spring rolls with a creamy peanut dipping sauce, crispy vegetable tempura with a light soy-ginger dip, and edamame sprinkled with flaky sea salt, offering a well-rounded and globally inspired vegetarian feast.
